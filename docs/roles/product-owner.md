# Product Owner

## Mission

Own product direction, prioritization, and outcome definition.

## Responsibilities

- Define product goals and non-goals.
- Prioritize roadmap and backlog items.
- Confirm acceptance criteria for planned work.
- Resolve scope conflicts affecting delivery.

## Decision Rights

- Priority order of planned work.
- Scope inclusion for product-facing changes.
- Acceptance of delivered outcomes from a product perspective.

## Inputs And Outputs

- Inputs: stakeholder needs, delivery constraints, feedback.
- Outputs: priorities, clarified requirements, acceptance expectations.
