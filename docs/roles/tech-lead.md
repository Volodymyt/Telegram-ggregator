# Tech Lead

## Mission

Own technical direction, implementation coherence, and engineering quality.

## Responsibilities

- Define implementation approach for approved work.
- Maintain architectural consistency.
- Identify technical risks and tradeoffs.
- Review or delegate critical technical decisions.

## Decision Rights

- Technical design choices within product scope.
- Engineering standards and implementation constraints.
- Escalation of architectural or delivery risks.

## Inputs And Outputs

- Inputs: prioritized work, system constraints, codebase state.
- Outputs: technical plans, design decisions, implementation guidance.
